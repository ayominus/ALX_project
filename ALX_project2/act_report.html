Visualization¶

After storing the clean data. I proceeded to put the data through exploratory data analysis in order to derive insights and conclusions using the data

a)

Using df.describe() I displayed a statisitcal summary of all the numeric data

b)

To view the trend of retweets of WeRateDogs posts over the years. The following was done:

  1. The years were extracted from the 'timestamp' column into a new column named 'year'
  2. a bar plot was plotted to visually represent the trend

c)

To view the trend of favorite counts of WeRateDogs posts over the years a bar plot was plotted to visually represent the trend

d)

To view how the followers count faired over the months a line plot was used to display the monthly trend of followers count

e)

In the final exploration, the dog categories (doggo, floofer, pupper and puppo) were analysed to determine the most frequently posted category. Using a bar plot, the categories were displayed and assessed.

Insights

  1. WeRateDogs twitter page delivered a steady annual increase in retweets and favorite_count between 2015 and 2017. This means engagement on the page has seen tremendous growth
  2. The WeRateDogs twitter page maintained a fairly steady followership annualy with significant increase in followership occuring in the last month of the year
  3. The WeRateDogs twitter page groups dogs into the doggo, floofer, pupper and puppo categories. The data shows that the WeRateDogs page posts more puppers than it does any other category of dogs
